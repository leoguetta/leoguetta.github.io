Léonard Guetta

Welcome

I am currently a post-doc at the Max-Planck-Institut für Mathematik in Bonn, Germany.

Before that, I did my PhD under the supervision of François Métayer and Clemens Berger at the IRIF laboratory of Université Paris-Cité, and graduated in January 2021.

For more details, you can look at my CV.

My main research interests are category theory, higher category theory and homotopy theory.
