Toto I'm a currently postdoc at at Utrecht University, in Paige North's group. As of January 2025, I will be also part of Ieke Moerdijk's group.

My main interests revolve around (higher) category theory and homotopy theory. In particular, I am interested in algebraic models of homotopically coherent higher structures.

I'm currently co-hosting the Category Theory Seminar at Utrecht University with Fernandu Chu Rivera.
