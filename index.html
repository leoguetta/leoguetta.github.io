Léonard Guetta

Welcome

I am currently a post-doc in Paige North's group at Utrecht University. Before that, I was a post-doc at the Max-Planck-Institut für Mathematik in Bonn, and even before that, I did my PhD under the supervision of François Métayer and Clemens Berger at the IRIF laboratory of Université Paris-Cité.

For more details, you can look at my CV.

My main research interests are higher category theory, category theory and homotopy theory.
